Built for the real bottleneck

The job is to keep hub movement explainable, not hide it.

01

Route parcels with context

Keyword and postcode matching helps pickup hubs assign parcels while preserving the route, agent, and match reasons.

02

Keep custody visible

Hub arrival, departure, warehouse, delivery, and payment events form a readable timeline for operators.

03

Give customers a safe view

Public tracking uses opaque tracking codes and redacted addresses while authenticated views enforce ownership.

How it flows

One path from pickup request to settled delivery.

1

Customer request

A customer submits pickup, dropoff, COD amount, and notes through a CSRF-protected form.

2

Hub assignment

Hub staff filter visible parcels and assign only routes and agents owned by their hub.

3

Agent run

Agents can update only parcels assigned to them, following allowed status transitions.

4

Settlement

COD parcels stay visible to authorized hubs until marked settled and logged as an event.
